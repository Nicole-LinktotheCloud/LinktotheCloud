## Overview

A client needed to move their document management out of email chains and shared drives and into a governed, searchable system inside Microsoft 365. The existing process had no version control, no approval tracking, and no reliable way to find the current version of any document.

**Work included:**

- SharePoint library design with structured metadata, content types, and managed taxonomy
- Automated approval workflows via Power Automate triggered on document submission
- Teams channel integration surfacing document notifications where staff already worked
- Access control and permission configuration by document type and department

The result: documents are findable, approvals are tracked, and staff aren't managing version history by hand.

**Outcome:** 5.0 rating · 46 hours delivered
